A person-centred recruitment hub that aims to increase inclusion in an ever-changing world.

Everyone should have access to the same career opportunities, but in reality, protected characteristics, life history, and disability are just some of the factors that create barriers to recruitment, retention and people being able to develop into the best version of themselves.

More2Offer provide a fresh and innovative approach to recruitment. We take pride in supporting individuals to explore, discover and highlight all of their positive attributes and to identify and address barriers that they may currently face in any stage of the employee cycle.

More2Offer is a simple, easy to use platform for prospective employees to highlight their positive attributes and unique skill-sets, allowing them to be matched with understanding, forward thinking employers, who value the roles individuals play in forming an organisation's culture and identity.

More2Offer supports and encourages employers to educate and empower themselves, encouraging recruitment from a truly diverse pool of candidates, focusing on “Can Do” rather than “May Struggle With”.

More2Offer invites employers to help us reshape the recruitment landscape.

Employers can advertise vacancies and browse prospective employee’s whose unique career and life journeys have provided them with a massively varied skills-set.

More2Offer encourages you to think outside the box. Do you really want to miss out on an A-Grade employee just because they have a different ability set than what you have previously been used to?

Taking the time to adapt your recruitment approach can make all the difference to someone who may need a bit of extra support…and the rewards of doing so can be nothing short of amazing.

Join us today as we revolutionise employment and make sure that once and for all, absolutely nobody gets left behind.
